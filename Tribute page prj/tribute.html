Steven "Steve" Paul Jobs

The Co-Founder of Apple Inc.

Here's the timeline of Steve Jobs' life:

  • 1955 "- Born in San Francisco, California"
  • 1968 "- In his 13, Jobs was given a summer job by Bill Hewlett of Hewlett-Packard"
  • 1972 "- Jobs enrolled at Reed College in Portland, Oregon, but one semester later, he dropped out without telling his parents."
  • 1974 "- Returned to his parents' home in Los Altos and began looking for a job."
  • 1975 "- Jobs and Wozniak attended meetings of the Homebrew Computer Club, which was a steppingstone to the development and marketing of the first Apple computer."
  • 1976 "- Jobs, Wozniak, and administrative overseer Ronald Wayne founded Apple Computer Company (now called Apple Inc.)."
  • 1977 "- Jobs and Wozniak introduced Apple II at the West Coast Computer Faire."
  • 1978 "- Jobs at age 23, Jobs was worth over $1 million (equivalent to $4.49 million in 2022). By age 25, his net worth grew to an estimated $250 million (equivalent to $805 million in 2022)."
  • 1985 "- Jobs resigned from Apple, and founded NeXT Inc. with $7 million."
  • 1986 "- funded the spinout of The Graphics Group (later renamed Pixar) from Lucasfilm's computer graphics division for the price of $10 million, $5 million of which was given to the company as capital and $5 million of which was paid to Lucasfilm for technology rights."
  • 1997 "- Return to Apple Inc."
  • 2001 "- Jobs was granted stock options in the amount of 7.5 million shares of Apple with an exercise price of $18.30."
  • 2003 "- Jobs was diagnosed with cancer"
  • 2011 "- Dies at the age of 56."
